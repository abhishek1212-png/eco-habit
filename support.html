❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my eco habits?
Open the app and tap any habit card to mark it as done for the day. Your streak and XP will update automatically.
How does the leaderboard work?
The leaderboard shows users who have opted in to share their username, XP, streak and level. You can choose to stay private when you first sign in.
How do I reset my streak?
Streaks reset automatically if you miss a day. To keep your streak going, complete at least one habit every day.
How do I delete my account?
Tap the 🗑 Delete button in the top right of the home screen. This will permanently delete all your data and cannot be undone.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. We'll send you a reset link.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your email is never shared publicly. Only your username, XP, streak and level appear on the leaderboard — and only if you choose to opt in.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Eco Habit collects your email address and username to create and manage your account. Your streak, XP, and habit data are stored securely via Firebase.

We do not sell your data to third parties. Leaderboard data (username, XP, streak, level) is only shared publicly if you explicitly consent when signing in.

To request deletion of your data, use the Delete Account feature in the app or email us at abhishek1212@icloud.com.
