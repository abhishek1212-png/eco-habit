🌿 Eco Habit — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Eco Habit ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Habit activity — the eco habits you log, your XP score, streak count, and reminder times. Stored in Firebase Firestore, linked to your account.
  • Notification identifiers — stored locally on your device only to manage scheduled reminders. Never sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To sync your habits and progress across devices.
  • To send local reminders you have scheduled.
  • To display your streak and XP progress.

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or health data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and cloud storage. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

5. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256) by Google Firebase. Passwords are never stored or logged by us — they are handled entirely by Firebase Authentication.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained until you delete your account. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at support@yourwebsite.com. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Eco Habit is rated 6+ and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes via the app.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@yourwebsite.com
